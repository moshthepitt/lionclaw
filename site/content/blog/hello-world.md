Hello.

This is the LionClaw blog.

Agents are going to matter. Probably a lot. And if they matter, we should not
quietly accept a future where a few giant companies own the agents, the memory,
the tools, the credentials, the workflows, and eventually the work itself.

We have seen this movie before. It was called Web 2.0, and it ended with
everyone renting their own digital lives back from platforms.

LionClaw is a small refusal of that future.

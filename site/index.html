Why LionClaw

An agent is not just chat.

It reads files. It writes code. It remembers decisions. It can use credentials. It can keep working after you leave.

That power should not need a landlord.

LionClaw gives it a local home.

Your project

It works where your code already lives.

Your memory

Sessions, drafts, and history stay on your machine.

Your credentials

Keys are handed to a run deliberately.

Your runs

Interactive work, background jobs, and scheduled tasks go through LionClaw.

Your record

Important actions leave something you can inspect later.

Agents will become the interface to everything. Do not let someone else own yours.
